Contemporary Art is simply all art that is produced today. By ‘today’ it means ‘within our lifetime’ with the understanding that lifespans vary from person to person. The term is often used interchangeably with Modern Art, but this is not correct . Modern Art is (and always will be) used to describe a particular period of art movement, specifically the 19th Century through to the 1960s (e.g. Impressionism, Cubism, Surrealism, Pop Art). ....
